About Me

My name is Isabella King, and I have over 6 years of experience in art and animation for the game industry. I specialize in 2D and 3D art, character design, and animation that breathe life into games.

From creating stylized characters to developing fluid animations that enhance player interaction, I focus on ensuring that every visual element of a game complements the gameplay experience. I work closely with designers and developers to maintain a consistent visual language that reflects the intended tone and atmosphere of the game.

My goal is to craft visually stunning and memorable assets that contribute to the storytelling and immersion in games, creating dynamic worlds that captivate players from start to finish.
